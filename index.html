Welcome!

In this experiment, you will have to go through 20 tests. The estimated duration is around 20 minutes.

Each test will present to you an image or a sound and offer you some way to manipulate it.

Then, there is a questionnaire regarding your reaction to it, namely the tension you felt. There is also a grid in which you must click on the cell that better suits your underlying emotional state when considering the image or sound, which we will use to place you on the arousal/valence spectrum.

Make sure your environment allows you to focus on the tests with no distractions. Please do this on a computer (no tablets/smartphones) and use headphones.

Once you get to the first sound test, set your audio to a volume that is comfortable to you for all the sounds displayed and keep it the same throughout the remaing tests. Play only one sound at a time.

Every time you finish a test, click on Next test! and another will be randomly chosen. Use only the buttons within the website. At the end, you will be able to manually select any test and update your answers if you want to. Save your answers at the end of every test!

Once you finish, Submit! You will be asked to send us your results.
