NOTICE: In order to get accurate results, you must use Google Chrome or Opera as your browser. Please use a computer (no tablets/smartphones) and headphones. Put the computer on a flat surface (desk/table) and sit properly on a chair in front of it at a distance of about 70cm. Make sure your environment allows you to focus on the tests with no distractions.

Before moving forward, please consider the following:

  • The participation is voluntary, you may opt-out at any point without any need for justification. In this case, any data collected on you would then be erased.
  • All data will be stored privately, in accordance with GDPR it will be destroyed after five years.
  • All the information collected will be processed for analysis, investigation and dissemination of results in scientific publications or conferences related to this project.

Please read attentively:

  • The estimated duration is around 30 minutes.
  • Firstly, you will be presented with some important information along with a sound volume control test and a quick questionnaire on some personal information.
  • Afterwards, there will be 22 tests. Each test will present to you one image/sound on the left and on right, they are the same image/sound. You will have to use the given sliders to manipulate the one the left to be the most tense possible and on the right to be the least tense possible. Underneath each there is a questionnaire regarding your reaction to it, namely the tension you felt. There is also a grid in which you must click on the cell that better suits your underlying emotional state when considering the image or sound.
  • The sound test includes a button which functions like a piano key, you hover (not click!) the mouse on it to begin the sound and it will play as long as your mouse is over the button, it will stop once you remove the mouse from over it.
  • Every time you finish a test, click on Next test! at the bottom of the page and another will be randomly chosen.
  • Use only the buttons within the website, you can not refresh the page or go back.
  • At the end, you will be able to manually select any test by clicking on the test number and update your answers if you want to. If you do this, save your answers at the end of every test!

By moving forward, you agree to participate in the study, having understood and agrees to all of its conditions.
