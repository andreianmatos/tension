Before agreeing to move forward in the study, please read the following information attentively:

  • The estimated duration is around 30 minutes. The participation is voluntary thus you may opt-out at any point, without any need for justification. Any data collected on you would then be erased.
  • Each test will present to you one image or one sound repeated twice, that is, the left and right image/sound are the same. You will have to use the given sliders to manipulate the image/sound on the left to be the most tense possible and on the right to be the least tense possible. Underneath each there is a questionnaire regarding your reaction to it, namely the tension you felt. There is also a grid in which you must click on the cell that better suits your underlying emotional state when considering the image or sound.
  • Make sure your environment allows you to focus on the tests with no distractions. Please use a computer (no tablets/smartphones) and headphones.
  • Every time you finish a test, click on Next test! at the bottom of the page and another will be randomly chosen. Use only the buttons within the website, no refreshing the page or going back.
  • At the end, you will be able to manually select any test and update your answers if you want to. If you do this, save your answers at the end of every test!
  • Once you submit your answers, you authorize the processing of the data collected within the context of this project for analysis, investigation and dissemination of results.
